Worldwide Gender Wage Gap (2005-2021)

This chart visualizes the gender wage gap across developed countries from 2005 to 2021, represented through an interactive line graph. Each line represents a country, showing the change in gender wage gap over the years, with interactive elements that allow the viewer to highlight or hide specific countries. Additionally, the graph provides tooltips that display detailed information, including the exact wage gap and a country's rank compared to others, at each data point on hover. Countries can be selected and de-selected at will.
